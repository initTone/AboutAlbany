Tulip Festival

Saturday, May 11 - Sunday, May 12 Albany Tulip Festival is one of the most popular annual events in Albany, NY that typically draws tens of thousands of people to Washington Park each year. The 2024 Albany Tulip Festival will take place over Mother's Day weekend on May 11 & 12 and feature all your favorite traditions, events, and activities. 1 New Scotland Ave Albany, NY

Washington Park

Located in the heart of Albany, NY, Washington Park is the city's main park and a Capital Region hotspot for outdoor activities, events, entertainment, and more. Spanning approximately 89 acres, it's one of the most beautifully sculpted and well-maintained parks around, making it a great place to visit during your time in Albany. State and Willet Streets Albany, NY 12210 (518) 434-5699 www.washingtonparkconservancy.org

Pine Hollow Arboretum

The Pine Hollow Arboretum is a 25-acre, member-supported, not-for-profit arboretum and nature preserve situated five miles from downtown Albany, in Slingerlands, New York. The arboretum's cataloged collection consists of over 3,300 unique trees, shrubs and other woody plants from around the world. Fees: Admission to the arboretum is FREE and open to the public 365 days a year, from dawn until dusk. 34 Pine Hollow Rd, Slingerlands, NY 12159 pinehollowarboretum.org (518) 992-2033

Ten Broeck Mansion

perched above Albany's Arbor Hill neighborhood, boasts stunning formal gardens. Cared for by the Cornell Cooperative Extension's Master Gardener Volunteer Program, the luscious gardens flourish throughout the spring and summer months. During the spring, tiptoe through the tulips and admire the daffodils, forget-me-nots, irises, lilacs, lily-of-the-valleys and more. During the dog days of summer, escape to the shady gardens to cool off among the roses, peonies, begonias and other blooms. The gardens are open for free from dawn to dusk. 9 Ten Broeck Place Albany, NY 12210 518-436-9826

Pruyn House

The Pruyn House, the country estate of Casparus Francis Pruyn, is tucked in Colonie. The idyllic grounds feature a lovely herb and flower garden maintained by the Fort Orange Garden Club. Designed to bloom from mid-May through Mid-October, the garden is filled with stunning flowers, bushes and shrubs and is surrounded by a charming white fence. Meander through the paths of the garden for a peaceful escape and to connect with nature. The grounds are open for free on Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM. 207 Old Niskayuna Rd Latham, NY 12110 Phone: (518) 783-1435 www.pruynhouse.org

Historic Cherry Hill

Historic Cherry Hill, located on South Pearl Street in Albany, originally owned by the Van Rensselaer family. Explore the reconstructed arbor and garden temples, as well as a formal flower garden, and the peony beds that date back to the 1930s. The grounds and gardens are open for free starting on June 18 on Fridays from 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. 523 1/2 South Pearl Street Albany, NY 12202 518-434-4791 historiccherryhill.org
